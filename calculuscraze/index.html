Introduction

The objective of this website is to provide online resources dedicated to teaching calculus. It is mainly focused on students but can also be interesting for anyone fascinated by mathematics and looking to expand their knowledge in the area of calculus. The resources are presented, especially through various YouTube channels specialized in teaching calculus, and also through reviews of books dedicated to calculus. Personally, I found learning calculus to be very challenging. Mastering calculus can be a difficult task, but with the right resources and materials, one can conquer the subject.

Why People Will Visit This Site? University students, especially those studying calculus, will find valuable information on this website to help them excel in their studies. They will come seeking guidance, recommendations, and reviews of YouTube channels and books related to calculus.

When students visit the website, they expect to find answers to various questions, such as: Which calculus YouTube channels offer clear explanations and engaging tutorials? What are the best calculus books that suit different learning styles and proficiency levels? Where can I find challenging but effective calculus exercises to practice my skills and reinforce my understanding?
