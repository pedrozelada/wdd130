Videos Lately, a wealth of information and tutorials has been created on the Internet. YouTube, in particular, hosts several teachers, students, and individuals who create videos explaining mathematics. One advantage of learning from these videos is the ability to learn at your own pace. If there is a concept not well understood, students can rewatch the videos, paying closer attention. The website's objective is to mention these channels, provide a brief history, highlight the quality of the content, and offer a review.
